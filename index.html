MEMORY SOUND

Sometimes, I seem have an unexplained connection with music, I learned many instruments when I was young, like piano, Chinese zither, Ukulele, etc. And this work was enlightened by a music performance, which made some annoy sounds into a unique song.

And I think...

Sound is always around us, big or small. Often we only respond to the voices we care about, such as conversations and musics? What about the voices we ignore? They may be waiting for our response.Like singer, Henry, he loves every single sound. In your busy life, have you stopped to listen to the voices around us? Do you hear the voice of urban renew and development? Are childhood songs remembered in your ears? I want to use this work to translate what happened in the community I once lived in with sound, and respond to my memory for this place.
